WAG-C3


The WAGC-3 also known as WAG-10, is a class of 25 kv AC electric locomotive rebuilt by Banaras Locomotive Works (BLW).
It can deliver 10,000 hp (7.457 kW). The locomotive was rebuilt from a WDG-3A diesel locomotive.
The name stands for broad gauge (W), alternating current (A), goods service (G), converted (C).
